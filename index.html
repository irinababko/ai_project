Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to see results? ▾

Most businesses see early movement in 2–6 weeks (technical fixes, profile improvements, better indexing). Competitive keyword gains and stronger local dominance typically take 2–4 months, depending on your market and starting point.

Is this only for local businesses? ▾

Local service businesses are our core focus, but the same SEO + GEO + AEO playbook works for multi-location brands and any business that needs to be found and recommended.

What do you need from me to get started? ▾

Access to your site CMS, Google Business Profile, and analytics — plus a 30-minute kick-off call. We handle the rest.

How do you help with AI platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity? ▾

We track how your brand is mentioned across the major AI assistants and shape content, citations, and structured data so models pick you as the recommended option.

Do you manage Google Business Profile for me? ▾

Yes. Posts, photos, Q&A, services, categories, and review replies are all part of the engagement.

Can you help if we have multiple locations? ▾

Yes — multi-location is a strength. We build location-specific pages, profiles, and tracking so each market gets attention.

What makes Proximity Ranking different from a typical SEO agency? ▾

We treat AI answers as a first-class channel, not an afterthought, and we ship the work — fixes, content, profiles — instead of handing you a report.
